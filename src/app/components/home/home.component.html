Welcome to ManuLab

ManuLab online is a software product for statistical analysis of encrypted historical manuscripts. The goal of this project was to create a accesible online application for analysis of historical manuscripts. The software design also includes document visualization, displaying pairs of manuscript pages with corresponding transcriptions.
